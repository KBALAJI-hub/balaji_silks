About Us

Balaji Silks is a family-owned house of traditional Indian textiles, bringing handcrafted sarees from skilled weavers to your wardrobe. We focus on sustainable sourcing, fair artisan pay, and preserving regional weaving techniques.

Our Story: Founded in 1998 by the Reddy family, Balaji Silks began as a small storefront committed to authentic handloom sarees. Over two decades we have grown into a trusted curator of regional weaves while maintaining close relationships with the weaving communities that create them.

What we do: We source directly from artisan clusters across South India and beyond ‚Äî Kanchipuram, Banaras, Gadwal, Pochampally and more. Each saree is inspected for weave quality, dye fastness, and finishing before it reaches our collection.

Our mission: To preserve traditional weaving techniques, provide sustainable livelihoods for artisans, and offer customers lasting pieces that celebrate heritage and craftsmanship.

Sustainability & Fair Trade: We prioritize natural fibers, low-impact dyes, and transparent pricing. We work with verified co-operatives and provide training and fair compensation programs to artisan partners.

Community impact: Balaji Silks runs outreach initiatives ‚Äî weaving workshops, micro-credit support for artisan families, and seasonal skill-development camps ‚Äî to strengthen the communities we depend on.

Visit us: Our flagship store is located in Bangalore. For wholesale inquiries, custom orders, or collaborations, email balajisilks16@gmail.com or call +91 6281748574.

Customer promise: Authenticity guaranteed, attentive returns & alterations, and personalised styling support for all celebrations.

Thank you for supporting handcrafted textiles ‚Äî every purchase helps keep a centuries-old craft alive.
